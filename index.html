City-breaks

Magical Marrakech

EL Badi Palace in Marrakech is a stunning example of Moroccan architecture and culture. Built in the 16th century, this palace is a testament to the grandeur of the Saadian Dynasty and the beauty of Moroccan art and design.

Rome

Rome is one of the most iconic and most-traveled cities in Europe, with a long history to match. Rome is also a foodie's heaven, from the world-class restaurants to casual trattorias and takeaway pizzas.
